Class: Spritesheet

Spritesheet(spriteSheetToLoad)

new Spritesheet(spriteSheetToLoad)

Responsible for controlling where in spritesheet sprite should render.
Currently, constructor serves as the factory for producing various types of sprites.
Sprite is tightly coupled to this.
